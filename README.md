# Cybersecurity & Social Engineering Awareness Notes

This repository contains learning notes related to cybersecurity awareness,
social engineering, and user-focused security.

The emphasis is on understanding why attacks like phishing work and how
human behavior plays a role in security incidents.

## Topics
- Phishing techniques (conceptual level)
- Common red flags in emails and messages
- How attackers manipulate trust and urgency
- Why users make mistakes
- Importance of awareness training

## Perspective
- Defensive and educational
- Human-centric security
- Awareness over exploitation

## What I learned
- Technical defenses alone are not enough
- Users are often the primary target
- Clear communication and training reduce risk
- Understanding attacker thinking improves defense

## Ethics
All content is educational.
No phishing templates, real attacks, or active infrastructure are included.
